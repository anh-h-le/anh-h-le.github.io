Overview

BBSTORE'S is a women's fashion brand in Vietnam focused on elegance and minimalism, targeting women aged 25–34 with higher incomes and modern lifestyles who value quality and individuality. The brand is seeking opportunities to improve and grow in fashion retail, particularly in boosting in-store sales revenue.

Data from 01/01/2024-05/26/2024 was collected and stored in an Excel file with two sheets:

  • Daily Sales: each row shows sales data for a single day.
  • Transaction Data: each row represents an individual transaction; multiple transactions may occur per day.

Recommendations

Sales were low early in the week and stronger toward the end.

------------------>

Boost sales on low days (Mon-Wed)

  • Discounts:
    • 5-10% off or buy-1-get-1 on accessories.
    • Discount on lowest-priced item when buying multiple items.
  • Flash sales:
    • Midday flash sales on the first Mon/Tue/Wed monthly to drive urgency.

Sales declined over time.

  • Revenue and traffic fell.
  • Conversion rose as traffic halved.
  • Target met in the last month but dropped from Jan-May.

------------------>

Use events and perks to increase traffic

  • Early access to new or limited collections.
  • Personalized gifts on special occasions.
  • Personal styling to upsell accessories.

Product categorization overlooked room for improvement.

  • Some “discontinued” items performed well with discounts.
  • Some non-discontinued items underperformed.

------------------>

Reassess discontinuation and underperformance

  • Test lower prices on discontinued items:
    • Sales improve: keep product at adjusted price.
    • No improvement: ask customers if urgency or visibility drove sales.
  • For weak items not discontinued: research and survey customers:
    • If quality issues: improve design or manufacturing/sourcing.
    • If low awareness: use newsletters, staff training, and ads.

Pricing was not justified by segmentation and positioning.

  • Customers are price-sensitive and avoid higher price points.

------------------>

Introduce product tiers

  • Essential: functional everyday pieces.
  • Signature: timeless minimalist upgrades.
  • Couture: limited, exclusive high-end pieces.

Key Recommendation for Sustainable Growth: Rebranding

Step 1: Analytics

  • Understand brand perception via customers' feedback, social media presence, and competition.

Step 2: Redefining/Reevaluating Brand's Mission and Values

  • Decide on where the brand is headed and corresponding unique selling points.

Step 3: Develop/Refresh the Signature Brand Aesthetic for Enhanced Visibility

  • Update the logo and colors to reflect the brand's direction.
  • Design branded packaging and gifts.

Step 4: Ensure Quality Consistency

  • Make sure product designs reflect the brand's image and values.
  • Monitor the production process to ensure high-quality finished goods.

Step 5: Invest in Brand Promotion

  • Sponsor fashion shows to enhance visibility of the brand.
  • Collab with celebrities that advocate for the brand's values.
